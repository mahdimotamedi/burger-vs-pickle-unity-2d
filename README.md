# 🍔 Hamburger vs Pickles 🥒

A funny 2D Unity game where a brave hamburger must survive against pickles coming from the right side, while collecting lettuce, cheese, and ketchup to become the ultimate burger!

## 🎮 About the Game

**Hamburger vs Pickles** is a colorful and silly 2D Unity game where you play as a cute hamburger.

Pickles come from the **right side of the screen** and move toward the hamburger. The player must avoid or fight the pickles while collecting tasty ingredients like **lettuce**, **cheese**, and **ketchup** that also appear from the right side.

The more ingredients you collect, the better your score becomes!

## ✨ Features

- Cute and cartoon-style 2D graphics
- Pickle enemies spawning from the right side
- Collectible ingredients:
  - Lettuce
  - Cheese
  - Ketchup
- Simple and smooth controls
- Score system
- Funny food-themed gameplay
- Great for learning Unity 2D mechanics

## 🕹️ Controls

| Key | Action |
|---|---|
| `↑` | Jump (Can hold for bigger jump) |
| `Space` | Jump / Restart |

## 🎯 Objective

Your goal is to:

1. Avoid the pickles coming from the right side.
2. Collect lettuce, cheese, and ketchup coming from the right side.
3. Increase your score by collecting ingredients.
4. Keep the hamburger alive as long as possible.
5. Beat your highest score.

## 🥒 Enemy

### Pickle

Pickles are the main enemies of the game.

- They spawn on the right side of the screen.
- They move toward the hamburger.
- If a pickle hits the hamburger, the player loses health or the game ends.
- The player must dodge or attack them.

## 🧀 Collectibles

### Lettuce

A fresh ingredient that gives points when collected.

### Cheese

A tasty ingredient that increases the score.

### Ketchup

A special ingredient.
